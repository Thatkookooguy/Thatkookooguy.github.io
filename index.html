Reo is a pop-disco-electronic duo, performed and produced by the musicians Zoe Polanski and Or Edri. The songs were written, produced and fully performed by Polanski and Edri, exclusively on analogue equipment from the 80's and 90's that were collected carefully to create the warm and unique sound of the debut album.

Behind the ensemble are many performances at leading Tel-Aviv clubs and festivals all over.

In her solo project, she stays close to the genre of Rio, but while the duo stroked with technicolor sensuality, Edri alone is a deeper submarine. Like in a magical dream, virtuoso guitarist's Omer Schneider sensitively weaves within his music an abundance of different musical genres.

After a number of tours in Europe with the glam rock band CHASE THE ACE, Omer decided to go on a new way and make his own art. With the trio Tamir Borsuk (Drums), Tal Shturm (Bass), and Neil Kalman (Saxophones), they create magical picturesque melodies, wrapped in a heavy rock spirit.
